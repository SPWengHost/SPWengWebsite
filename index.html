Warehouse Modification – Dragon Arrow (Pvt.) Ltd.

Year: 2025
Type: Industrial · Renovation & Retrofit
Location: Wellampitiya, Sri Lanka
Scope: Structural assessment, modification design, documentation

SPW was appointed to redesign an existing warehouse to accommodate new production machinery for Dragon Arrow (Pvt.) Ltd. The project required major internal reconfiguration while maintaining the existing envelope and operational constraints.

Scope of Work

  • Structural assessment of existing warehouse and load paths.
  • Design of new roof trusses and support members for clear-span machinery areas.
  • Design for removal of internal walls and redistribution of loads.
  • Construction documentation and detailing for structural modifications.

Engineering Highlights

  • Sequenced modification strategy to minimise downtime and disruption to operations.
  • Optimised steel truss layout to achieve required clear heights within existing geometrical constraints.
  • Enhanced structural performance while respecting existing foundations and supports.
  • Clear, buildable drawings to guide contractors through phased strengthening works.

Residential House – Ahangama

Year: 2025
Type: Residential · New Build
Location: Ahangama, Sri Lanka
Scope: Architectural and structural design, project management

SPW provided full architectural and structural consultancy for a three-storey coastal residence in Ahangama. The design harmonises open-plan living spaces with robust structural solutions suited to the coastal environment.

Scope of Work

  • Architectural concept development and detailed design.
  • Structural design and analysis of frames, slabs and foundations.
  • Preparation of construction drawings and reinforcement detailing.
  • Overall project management and coordination with contractors.

Engineering Highlights

  • Efficient structural grid aligned with architectural planning and services routes.
  • Durable detailing and material choices for coastal exposure conditions.
  • Balanced structural stiffness for comfort under wind actions.
  • Close coordination between design and site teams ensuring smooth delivery.

Commercial Building Renovation – Weligama

Year: 2024
Type: Commercial · Vertical Extension
Location: Weligama, Sri Lanka
Scope: Structural and architectural consultation, strengthening design

The client sought to add two additional floors to an existing two-storey commercial building. SPW was engaged to provide structural and architectural input for the safe vertical extension and reconfiguration.

Scope of Work

  • Assessment of existing structure, foundations and allowable capacities.
  • Structural design for additional storeys and strengthening measures.
  • Architectural layout optimisation for new floors and circulation.
  • Construction documentation and detailing for extension works.

Engineering Highlights

  • Feasibility study to validate safe vertical expansion options.
  • Strengthening strategy to support new loads within site and cost constraints.
  • Integration of new elements with minimal disruption to existing tenants.
  • Practical detailing to enable phased construction and safe operations.

Residential Renovation – Wattala

Year: 2024
Type: Residential · Renovation
Location: Wattala, Sri Lanka
Scope: Structural design, roof structure, cost estimation

SPW was commissioned to extend an existing residential building and design a new roof structure at rooftop level, adding usable area while retaining the existing fabric where possible.

Scope of Work

  • Structural design for rear extension and new rooftop structure.
  • Evaluation of existing building capacities and connection details.
  • Preparation of reinforcement details and construction drawings.
  • Cost estimation and quantity take-offs to support budgeting.

Engineering Highlights

  • Careful integration of new loads with the existing structural system.
  • Buildable solutions designed around constrained access and an occupied house.
  • Cost-conscious design aligned with client budgets and phasing.
  • Improved usability and value of the property without full reconstruction.

About SPW Engineering Consultants

SPW is a dynamic consultancy providing architectural and structural design as well as engineering services for domestic and commercial clients in Sri Lanka.

Based in Colombo, SPW Engineering Consultants offers tailored engineering solutions that combine safety, functionality and aesthetics. With more than a decade of combined professional experience, our team supports projects from early feasibility through to construction support.

We work across residential, commercial and industrial sectors, including new builds, renovations and complex retrofits. Each project is approached with careful analysis, clear communication and a strong focus on buildability and long-term performance.

Vision · Mission · Values

Vision: To be a leading consultancy providing innovative, sustainable and high-quality architectural and structural solutions that enhance the built environment.

Mission: To deliver exceptional architectural and structural design services that ensure safety, functionality and aesthetic excellence. We strive to exceed expectations through expert consultancy, innovation and a commitment to engineering excellence for domestic and commercial clients.

  1. Initial Consultation – Understanding project objectives, constraints and budget.

Services

SPW provides a complete range of structural and architectural consultancy services for residential, commercial and industrial projects, tailored to statutory requirements, site conditions and your performance objectives.

Service 01
Structural Design & Analysis

We develop safe, economical and buildable structural systems using industry-standard design codes and advanced analysis tools.

Key benefits: Optimised member sizes and layouts, reduced material waste, clear drawings and calculations.

Applications: New residential, commercial and industrial buildings, multi-storey and long-span structures.

Service 02
Site Investigations

Comprehensive investigations of ground conditions, existing structures and site constraints to inform safe and efficient designs.

Key benefits: Informed design decisions, reduced risk, better cost and programme certainty.

Applications: New developments, extensions, pre-purchase due diligence.

Service 03
Construction Documentation

Detailed drawings, specifications and tender documents that communicate design intent clearly to contractors.

Key benefits: Fewer RFIs, reduced rework and strong basis for accurate pricing.

Applications: Architectural/structural drawings, reinforcement detailing, BOQs and specifications.

Service 04
Structural Inspections

Inspection and assessment of existing buildings to evaluate safety, durability and code compliance.

Key benefits: Clear understanding of condition, practical recommendations, support for approvals.

Applications: Condition surveys, pre/post-occupancy inspections, post-damage assessments.

Service 05
Retrofitting & Rehabilitation

Engineering solutions to strengthen, stabilise or extend the life of existing structures.

Key benefits: Extend building life, improve safety and performance, cost-effective alternative to new build.

Applications: Additional floors, upgrades for older buildings, post-deterioration repairs.

Service 06
Project Management

Coordination and oversight of project scope, time, cost and quality from planning through completion.

Key benefits: Single point of contact, reduced delays and variations, clear tracking of progress.

Applications: Residential and commercial projects, renovations with live operations.

Service 07
Seismic & Wind Load Analysis

Specialised analysis of structural behaviour under seismic and wind actions to ensure resilience and serviceability.

Key benefits: Code-compliant designs, reduced risk of damage, optimised lateral systems.

Applications: Mid-/high-rise buildings, industrial warehouses, structures in high wind/seismic areas.

Service 08
Value Engineering

Systematic review and optimisation of designs to balance safety, performance and cost.

Key benefits: Reduced material and construction costs, simplified construction, improved lifecycle value.

Applications: Review of preliminary and third-party designs, projects with tight budgets or logistics.
